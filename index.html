Features

Welcome to the Feature Section of Nutri-Map, your ultimate destination for all Diet, Nutrition and Wellness.

Personalized Diet Plans

Receive a tailored nutrition plan designed specifically for your body and goals. Our certified nutritionists will consider your unique needs, dietary preferences, and health conditions to create a plan that suits you best.

Meal Planning

Access a vast collection of delicious and healthy Diet Suggestions tailored to your needs. Our Chat-Bot will also create personalized meal plans, making it easier for you to stay on track and enjoy nutritious meals.

Interactive Chatbot

A smart, AI-powered chatbot to provide instant responses to nutrition-related queries. Helps users plan balanced meals and get advice on healthy eating habits.

Nutritional Information

Access detailed nutritional data about ingredients and meals in real time. Explore detailed nutritional data about meals and ingredients to make informed choices. Visual graphs and charts to track daily intake of proteins, carbs, fats, etc.
